研究生 Students

我們每年都會招收新的研究生。We welcome new graduate students to join our lab each year.

如果您是目前或新入學的學生，對開發人工智慧和機器學習方法和/或使用人工智慧推進治療和醫學感興趣，請直接向王昭能發送電子郵件。包括您的簡歷和您的研究興趣的簡要描述。 我們正在招募多個研究生課程的博士生，包括人工智慧在醫學、生物資訊學和綜合基因組學、生物醫學資訊學、計算生物學、數據科學在生物醫學、產學系計畫以及亞洲大學的其他項目。

If you are a current or newly admitted student interested in developing AI and machine learning methods and/or applying AI to advance therapies and medicine, please email Professor Wang Zhaoneng directly. Include your resume and a brief description of your research interests. We are currently recruiting PhD students for several graduate programs, including AI in medicine, bioinformatics and integrative genomics, biomedical informatics, computational biology, data science in biomedicine, industry-academia collaboration programs, and other projects at Asia University.
